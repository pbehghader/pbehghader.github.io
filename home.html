Pooyan Behnamghader

Pooyan is a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at the University of Southern California. He works in Software Architecture Research Group under the supervision of Prof. Nenad Medvidovic at the Center for Systems and Software Engineering.

His major areas of interest are:

  • Software Architecture Recovery, Software Repository Mining
  • Distributed Systems, Cloud Computing

He received his B.S. in Computer Engineering from the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Tehran under the supervision of Dr. Fatemeh Ghassemi in June 2013. He was a visiting PhD student in Software Architecture and Evolution Lab, led by Prof. Harald Gall, at the University of Zurich for the summer of 2015. In addition, He worked as a research assistant intern in the School of Computer and Communication at EPFL under the supervision of Prof. Claude Petitpierre in summer 2012.

Pooyan has some startup experiences. He co-founded Ambermelon Smart Watch (2015-2016) and Huntus Sharing Economy Platform (2015); and he worked with Basir Tech (2007).
